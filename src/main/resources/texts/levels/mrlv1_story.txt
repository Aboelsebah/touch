The Police Department in New York receives a call by worried neighbours. Their neighbour John Fitzgerald has not been around for a week and the neighbours are certain that he did not go on a vacation.
Moreover, mail is filling up his doorstep.
#pictureWindow2
Half an hour later two policemen arrive at Mr Fitzgerald's door. After ringing the bell and knocking extensively, they decide to open the door.
The door uses a new security system which is based on the Initials of the owner F and J with spaces in a specific order.
Now it is your turn to help them to crack the code!
